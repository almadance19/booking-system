Your Dance Transformation

From First Steps to Soulful Expression at Alma Dance Frankfurt

1

Level 1: The Foundation

Months 1-2

Goal: Rhythm & Basic Geometry

Master the fundamental footwork and the "connection" that makes Latin dance special. You'll learn to hear the beat and move with a partner comfortably.

Tip: Don't worry about "looking good" yet. Everyone is a beginner here. You are building the roots!
2

Level 2: The Improvers

Months 3-6

Goal: Flow & Communication

Expand your vocabulary of moves. Here we focus on smooth transitions and "Leading & Following" without thinking about your feet.

The "Dip" Alert: This is where most people quit! It's the "messy middle." Keep goingâ€”you are just weeks away from it feeling natural.
3

Level 3: Intermediate

Months 7-12

Goal: Social Mastery & Styling

Now we add "flavor." You'll learn body isolation, arm styling, and how to adapt your dance to different speeds of music.

Reward: You are now a "Social Dancer." You can walk into any party in Frankfurt and dance with anyone with confidence!
4

Level 4: Adv. Intermediate +

12+ Months

Goal: Artistry & Expression

This is where "Alma" (Soul) happens. We work on musicality, complex turn patterns, and finding your unique personal style.

Growth: At this stage, you aren't just doing steps; you are telling a story with your body.

Why our Methodology works
