Dance is what makes us happy and gives us energy for the day.

We try to bring that to our classes and create a learning environment that is fun. We want to be the place where people make friends, feel comfortable and find balance to their working/student life.

About Us

Hi! We are Eduardo and Natalia, the founders of Alma Dance. We are a couple from Mexico and Slovakia living in Frankfurt.

After a several years teaching latin dance, we started this project to give latin dance classes and build a strong dance community.

And so Alma Dance came to life, representing the way we see dance and the place it has in our lives (in Spanish, “alma” means “soul”).

As professional dance teachers, we teach both locally and internationally.
