Why Reggaeton?

Reggaeton is a high-energy urban dance style that originated in Puerto Rico and has taken the world by storm.

Combining elements of hip-hop, Latin rhythms, and Caribbean dance styles, Reggaeton is known for its sensual movements, body isolations, and infectious energy.

At Alma Dance Frankfurt, our Reggaeton classes will teach you the fundamentals of this popular dance style while helping you build confidence, improve coordination, and have fun in a welcoming environment.

Whether you're a complete beginner or have some dance experience, our classes are designed to help you master the basics while learning choreography to popular Reggaeton hits.

Frankfurt's dance scene is embracing Reggaeton, and our classes offer the perfect opportunity to join this growing movement while getting a great workout and meeting new people.

Benefits of Reggaeton

  • Full-body workout

    Reggaeton dancing provides an excellent cardiovascular workout while toning muscles throughout your entire body. The dynamic movements engage your core, legs, and arms for a complete fitness experience.

  • Improved coordination

    Learning Reggaeton's intricate footwork and body isolations significantly enhances your coordination, rhythm, and body awareness.

  • Stress relief

    The high-energy nature of Reggaeton dancing releases endorphins that help reduce stress and boost your mood. It's a fun way to let loose and express yourself.

  • Increased confidence

    Mastering Reggaeton moves and performing choreography builds self-confidence both on and off the dance floor.

  • Social connections

    Our Reggaeton classes create opportunities to meet like-minded people who share your passion for dance and Latin culture.
