Alma Dance SEO Improvement Plan

1. Technical SEO Fixes

  • Fix Hero Video: Ensure a "Poster Image" is set so users don't see "Your browser does not support HTML5 video."
  • Optimize Images: Compress all gallery images to under 200KB using WebP format.
  • Mobile-First Check: Test the booking button size—ensure it's easy to click with a thumb.

2. Content Strategy (Bachata Focus)

Instead of a new domain, optimize /bachata-classes with these keywords:

Target Keywords: Bachata Frankfurt, Bachata Tanzschule Bornheim, Bachata für Anfänger, Latin Dance Community Frankfurt.
  • Add Local Context: Mention proximity to "Bornheim Mitte" and "Berger Straße."
  • FAQ Section: Add "Do I need a partner for Bachata in Frankfurt?" to catch "People Also Ask" traffic.
  • Video Alt-Text: Label your videos: "Bachata course for beginners in Frankfurt Bornheim".

3. Local Authority (Off-Page)

  • NAP Consistency: Ensure Name, Address, and Phone are 100% identical on Google Maps, Facebook, and your footer.
  • Incentivize Reviews: Ask students to mention the word "Bachata" or "Frankfurt" in their Google reviews.
  • Local Citations: List the school on Frankfurt-Tipp or local expat directories.

Pro Tip: The "English" Edge

Since you already have an English version, ensure your <html lang="en"> and <html lang="de"> tags are correctly set on their respective pages. This tells Google which version to show to expats searching in English vs locals searching in German.
