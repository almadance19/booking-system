The "Expat Hack": Why Dancing is the Fastest Way to Make Friends

Moving to the German financial hub is exciting, but the "Mainhattan" lifestyle can feel anonymous. At Alma Dance Frankfurt, we help you break the ice. Our studio isn't just a school; it's a global family where 120+ nationalities meet, connect, and express themselves through movement.

Join Our International Community

Whether you are a professional at the ECB, a student, or a digital nomad, you’ll find like-minded people here. Our classes are taught in a mix of English and German, making it the perfect environment for English speakers to feel at home while integrating into Frankfurt life.

No Dance Partner? Perfect!

Many people hesitate to join a dance school because they don't have a partner. At Alma Dance, 90% of our students sign up alone.

  • Frequent Rotation: We switch partners every few minutes. This speeds up your learning and ensures you meet everyone in the room.
  • Beginner-Friendly: Our "Soul-First" methodology focuses on rhythm and connection, so you feel confident even if you have two left feet.

Bachata, Salsa & Zouk in the Heart of Frankfurt

Located just steps away from Hauptwache and Konstablerwache, we offer the most central dance experience in the city. Perfect for an after-work de-stress session or a weekend workshop.
