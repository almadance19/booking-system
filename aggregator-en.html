Purpose of these Conditions

This document outlines the participation conditions for clients attending Alma Dance Frankfurt via aggregator platforms such as Wellpass or Gympass. It ensures fair access to limited class space and encourages commitment to learning and regular participation.

We believe dancing is an amazing journey of personal expression and growth, but it also requires commitment. True progress is achieved through consistent practice, and our school is structured to ensure the highest quality of instruction when students are dedicated to the process. Therefore, we can guarantee tangible results for clients who participate regularly and engage fully with our program.
