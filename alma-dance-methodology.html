Our Teaching Methodology

Progressive Learning

Structured curriculum that builds from foundational moves to advanced combinations, ensuring solid understanding at each level.

Social Practice

Weekly social dance events to practice with different partners and apply what you've learned in a real dance environment.

Personalized Feedback

Small class sizes with individual attention from instructors to correct technique and accelerate your progress.

Musicality Training

Learn to connect with the music, understand rhythms, and express yourself through movement to different Latin music styles.

Your 12-Month Dance Journey

Level 1

Basics Foundation

2 Months

Master fundamental steps, basic timing, and partner connection. Learn simple turns and basic footwork for all four dance styles.

  • Basic steps & timing for Bachata, Salsa, Zouk & Kizomba
  • Proper posture and frame
  • Introduction to Latin music rhythms
  • Social dance etiquette
Level 2

Improvers Development

4 Months

Expand your vocabulary with more turns, combinations, and styling. Develop better lead/follow techniques and musicality.

  • Multiple turn patterns for each dance style
  • Basic styling elements for followers
  • Introduction to musicality and hitting breaks
  • Transitioning between different dance styles
Level 3

Intermediate Skills

6 Months

Refine technique, learn more complex patterns, and develop personal style. Focus on smooth transitions and advanced musicality.

  • Advanced combinations and footwork variations
  • Body movement and isolations
  • Advanced leading/following techniques
  • Dance improvisation skills
Level 4

Intermediate-Advanced Mastery

6+ Months

Master complex combinations, develop your unique style, and prepare for performance-level dancing. Optional performance team participation.

  • Performance choreography elements
  • Advanced musical interpretation
  • Styling workshops and personal expression
  • Mentoring opportunities for newer students
