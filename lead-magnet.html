1. The "Grand Slam" Offer

  • High Perceived Value: Looks expensive but costs you little
  • Immediate Results: Client gets a "win" in first session
  • Low Time Investment: Under 60 minutes to deliver
  • Scales Naturally: Can be delivered to groups

2. Value Stacking

  • Tier 1: Free workshop (lead magnet)
  • Tier 2: Low-cost course ($197-297)
  • Tier 3: High-ticket program ($997-1997)
  • Tier 4: Mastermind/Group Coaching ($5k+)

3. The Perfect Webinar Structure

  • Minute 0-5: Big promise & social proof
  • Minute 5-20: Teach one valuable concept
  • Minute 20-35: Show transformation
  • Minute 35-55: Present offer & bonuses
  • Minute 55-60: Q&A & urgency close

4. Psychological Triggers

  • Scarcity: Limited spots available
  • Urgency: Offer expires in X hours
  • Social Proof: "Join 532 other dancers"
  • Authority: Certifications, years of experience
  • Reciprocity: Give massive value first
